# Privacy Policy for Neomedia

**Effective Date:** June 23, 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community by using the Neomedia application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice or our practices with regard to your personal information, please contact us.

This Privacy Policy describes how Neomedia collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our App.

## 1. Core Functionality & Sensitive Permissions
Neomedia is a utility application designed to help users manage their media visibility on their local device by creating `.nomedia` files in selected directories. 

To provide this core functionality, Neomedia requires the following sensitive permission:
* **MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (All Files Access):** This permission is strictly necessary for Neomedia to function. It allows the App to scan your device's internal storage and external storage (such as SD cards or USB OTG) to display your folder structure. It also allows the App to write a `.nomedia` file into the specific directories you choose, which instructs the Android OS to hide the media content of that folder from gallery applications.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
**We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data or files.**
* **Local Processing:** All directory scanning, file reading, and file writing operations (such as creating the `.nomedia` file) occur locally and completely offline on your device.
* **No Remote Servers:** We do not operate any remote servers, nor do we upload your file names, folder structures, photos, videos, or any other personal media to the internet.
* **No Analytics or Tracking:** Neomedia does not use third-party analytics, trackers, or advertising SDKs. Your app usage behavior is not monitored or logged.

## 3. Third-Party Access
Since we do not collect any personal data or files, we do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with any third parties. Neomedia operates entirely as a standalone, offline utility on your Android device.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since the App operates entirely offline and does not collect any data, there is no risk of children's data being transmitted or stored by us.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy or the Neomedia app, do not hesitate to contact the developer.
